Frequently Asked Questions About Veneers

How much do porcelain veneers typically cost at Jonathan A. Morris D.D.S., P.C. in Bethesda, MD?
At our Bethesda office, porcelain veneers usually range from $1,200 to $1,600 per tooth. The total cost depends on the number of veneers you need and the complexity of your case. We’ll provide a personalized estimate during your consultation and discuss available payment options and financing plans. Please note that most dental insurance plans do not cover cosmetic veneers.
How long does it take to get veneers from start to finish?
The entire veneer process at our Bethesda practice typically takes two to three appointments over two to three weeks. Your first visit includes a consultation and smile assessment. At the second appointment, we’ll prepare your teeth and take digital impressions for the dental lab. Your custom veneers are placed and adjusted during your final visit. We do our best to accommodate busy schedules and can discuss timing that works for you.
Is Dr. Jonathan A. Morris licensed and experienced in cosmetic dentistry procedures like veneers?
Yes, Dr. Morris is fully licensed to practice dentistry in Maryland and has over 20 years of experience, including extensive training in cosmetic procedures such as veneers. What is the process for getting veneers at your Bethesda dental office?
Our veneer process starts with a thorough consultation to discuss your goals and evaluate your dental health. We use digital imaging to preview your new smile. If you’re a good candidate, we’ll gently prepare your teeth by removing a thin layer of enamel, then take impressions for our partner dental lab. Temporary veneers are placed until your custom ones are ready. At your final appointment, we bond the permanent veneers and make any final adjustments to ensure a comfortable, natural fit.
