Frequently Asked Questions About Teeth Whitening

How much does professional teeth whitening cost at Jonathan A. Morris D.D.S., P.C. in Bethesda?
At our Bethesda office, professional teeth whitening typically ranges from $400 to $650, depending on the method and extent of treatment required. We offer both in-office and take-home whitening options, and during your consultation, Dr. Morris will provide a personalized quote based on your needs.
How long does the teeth whitening process take, and will I need multiple visits?
Our in-office whitening procedure usually takes about 90 minutes and can often be completed in a single visit. If you choose our custom take-home trays, you'll wear them daily for 1–2 weeks. Dr. Morris will discuss your goals and recommend the best approach during your appointment.
Is Jonathan A. Morris D.D.S., P.C. licensed to perform teeth whitening in Maryland?
Yes, Dr. Jonathan A. Morris is a licensed dentist in the state of Maryland with over 20 years of experience providing cosmetic dental services, including teeth whitening. Our practice follows all state regulations and uses FDA-approved whitening products for your safety.
What can I expect during the teeth whitening appointment?
At your appointment, we'll start by discussing your dental history and examining your teeth to ensure whitening is appropriate for you. For in-office whitening, we'll protect your gums and apply a professional-strength whitening gel, which is activated with a special light. The process is comfortable and takes about 90 minutes. You'll see immediate results, and we'll provide aftercare tips to help maintain your new smile.
Do you guarantee results, and what happens if I'm not satisfied with my teeth whitening?
While individual results can vary due to factors like tooth coloration or previous dental work, most patients see noticeable improvement after treatment. Dr. Morris will review your expectations beforehand and recommend the best option for your teeth. If you're not satisfied with the results, please let us know—we're committed to your satisfaction and will discuss possible touch-ups or alternative solutions.
