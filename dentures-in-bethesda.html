Frequently Asked Questions About Dentures

How much do dentures typically cost at Jonathan A. Morris D.D.S., P.C. in Bethesda, MD?

How long does the denture process usually take from start to finish?
The complete denture process usually takes 4 to 6 weeks at our Bethesda practice. This includes the initial examination, impressions, several fittings for proper adjustment, and delivery of your final dentures. We schedule multiple appointments to ensure comfort and a natural look, and we can accommodate urgent cases whenever possible.
Is Dr. Jonathan A. Morris licensed and experienced in denture services?
Yes, Dr. Jonathan A. Morris is a licensed dentist in Maryland with over 20 years of experience providing denture services. He stays current with the latest techniques and materials, and is a member of both the American Dental Association and the Maryland State Dental Association.
What is the process for getting new dentures at your Bethesda office?
Our denture process starts with a comprehensive oral exam and a discussion about your goals. We then take detailed impressions of your mouth, select tooth shape and shade with your input, and create a custom wax model for you to try. Once you’re satisfied with the fit and appearance, your final dentures are fabricated and adjusted for comfort. We offer follow-up visits to ensure proper fit and function.

Absolutely. We provide a one-year warranty on our custom dentures, covering adjustments and repairs for fit or manufacturing issues at no extra charge. If you experience discomfort or changes in fit, just contact our Bethesda office and we’ll arrange a prompt adjustment to ensure your satisfaction.
